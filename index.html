Earth's Energy Imbalance
Monthly · ~60 day lag
Net rate at which energy enters the Earth system, measured in watts per square metre. A positive imbalance means Earth is absorbing more energy than it emits — the root cause of warming. This has roughly doubled since 2000, driving accelerating ocean heat content.

Arctic Sea Ice Extent
Daily · annual index
Annual minimum Arctic sea ice extent (September). Arctic sea ice acts as Earth's "air conditioner" by reflecting sunlight. Its loss accelerates warming through the ice-albedo feedback: darker ocean absorbs more heat than reflective ice. March 2025 set a record lowest winter maximum.
