Earth's Energy Imbalance
Monthly · ~60 day lag
Net rate at which energy enters the Earth system (W/m²). A positive imbalance means Earth absorbs more than it emits — the root cause of warming. Has roughly doubled since 2000, driving accelerating ocean heat uptake and sea level rise.

Arctic Sea Ice Extent
Daily · annual index
Annual September minimum Arctic sea ice extent (M km²). Arctic ice acts as Earth's reflective shield — its loss triggers the ice-albedo feedback where darker ocean absorbs more heat. The oldest, thickest ice (>4yr old) has declined over 95% since the 1980s.
